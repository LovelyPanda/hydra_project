Detailed Description

Represents a terrain's chunk.

Chunk contains data needed to render square piece of terrain surface. It stores indices, their level of detail and level of detail number for vertices. Chunk represents only 1 level of detail contrary to hydra::data::TerrainFragment.

Warning:
maximum number of indexed primitives is 65K!
