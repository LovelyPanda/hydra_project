Detailed Description

Struct represents square fragment of terrain surface.

Terrain heightmap is splitted into square fragments upon load. Each fragment may contain several levels of detail and even some hierarchical structure of LODs. This struct represents such square. It contains different LODs of vertices (with flags of whether they are loaded), hierarchy of chunks (with same flags).
